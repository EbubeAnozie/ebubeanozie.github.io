About

I am pursuing a PhD in Applied Mathematics at the University of Colorado, Boulder. I obtained a Bachelor of Technology in Mathematics from the Federal University of Technology, Owerri, Nigeria where I emerged the Overall Best Graduating Student of the University’s Class of 2022.

Interests & Research

I am passionate about the use of computers in mathematics, particularly in numerical analysis. My research focuses on the development and optimization of numerical algorithms for large-scale systems aiming to improve scalability, robustness, and efficiency in simulating complex phenomena in science and engineering.
